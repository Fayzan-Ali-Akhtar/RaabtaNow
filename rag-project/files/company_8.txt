Tesla's mission is "to accelerate the world’s transition to sustainable energy." 
It values innovation, agility, environmental stewardship, and bold risk-taking. 
Tesla’s work culture is intense, fast-moving, and deeply mission-driven, requiring 
employees to work hard, move fast, and adapt quickly to change. As a major player in 
automotive and energy innovation, Tesla’s achievements include mainstreaming electric 
vehicles with products like the Model 3, building massive Gigafactories for efficient 
production, and making strides in autonomous driving, solar energy, and battery technology.
