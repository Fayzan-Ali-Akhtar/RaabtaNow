Google (Alphabet Inc.) is one of the world’s leading technology companies with a mission 
"to organize the world’s information and make it universally accessible and useful." 
Guided by values like user focus, innovation, transparency, and doing the right thing, 
Google has built a global culture known for openness, collaboration, and creativity. 
Employees are encouraged to pursue ambitious ideas in a flexible, supportive environment. 
Google operates across search, cloud computing, AI, and advertising, and has major achievements 
including creating the world’s dominant search engine, leading the Android mobile revolution, and 
pioneering AI research through DeepMind and products like Gemini.
