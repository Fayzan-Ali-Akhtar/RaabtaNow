Microsoft aims "to empower every person and every organization on the planet to achieve more." 
It values innovation, inclusivity, social responsibility, and a customer-first approach. 
Microsoft’s work culture promotes learning, mentorship, collaboration, and making a tangible 
societal impact, offering employees a chance to work across diverse products with global reach. 
As a leader in software, cloud computing, and gaming, Microsoft’s achievements include building 
the Windows and Office software empires, becoming a key cloud provider through Azure, and expanding 
into professional and gaming networks with acquisitions like LinkedIn and Activision Blizzard.
