NVIDIA seeks "to solve the world’s most challenging computational problems" through innovation and 
excellence. Its values include deep intellectual honesty, team collaboration, and pushing the 
boundaries of what’s possible. NVIDIA's work culture is built for people passionate about solving 
difficult problems and working on cutting-edge technology, especially in AI, graphics, and 
high-performance computing. Operating within the semiconductor and AI industry, NVIDIA’s major 
achievements include inventing the GPU, dominating AI hardware markets with products like the A100 
and H100 chips, and leading efforts in areas like autonomous driving and Omniverse virtual 
environments.
