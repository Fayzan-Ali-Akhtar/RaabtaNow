Apple is committed "to bringing the best user experience to its customers through innovative hardware,
software, and services." The company’s values include a deep emphasis on innovation, simplicity, 
excellence, privacy, and environmental responsibility. Apple fosters a creative, secretive, yet
highly collaborative culture where employees are encouraged to challenge norms and deliver 
perfection. A global leader in consumer electronics, software, and services, Apple’s achievements 
include introducing iconic products like the iPhone, MacBook, and Apple Watch, setting the gold 
standard for design, privacy advocacy, and consistently reshaping entire industries.
