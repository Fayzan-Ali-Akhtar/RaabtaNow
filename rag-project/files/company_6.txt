Netflix operates under the mission "to entertain the world," with values rooted in freedom and 
responsibility, innovation, and passion for storytelling. The company’s work culture emphasizes 
autonomy, radical transparency, and very high performance expectations, allowing individuals 
substantial freedom while expecting strong ownership of their work. As a leader in streaming 
entertainment, Netflix’s major achievements include revolutionizing television consumption with 
on-demand streaming, introducing binge-watching culture, and producing globally acclaimed original 
content such as Stranger Things, The Witcher, and The Crown.
