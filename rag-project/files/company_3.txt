Amazon’s mission is "to be Earth’s most customer-centric company, where customers can find 
and discover anything they might want to buy online." Its core values revolve around customer 
obsession, operational excellence, and innovation. Amazon’s work culture is famously fast-paced 
and high-intensity, with a strong emphasis on data-driven decision-making and leadership principles 
guiding everyday actions. Operating in industries like e-commerce, cloud computing, logistics, and 
AI, Amazon’s major achievements include revolutionizing global shopping habits, establishing AWS as 
the world’s largest cloud service, and setting new standards in logistics, fulfillment, and consumer 
technology like Alexa.
