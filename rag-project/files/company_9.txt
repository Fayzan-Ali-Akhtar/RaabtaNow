Adobe is guided by its mission "to change the world through digital experiences," placing values 
like genuine engagement, creativity, innovation, and inclusion at the center of its culture. Adobe’s 
work culture encourages creative freedom, employee well-being, and work-life balance, making it one 
of the most admired companies to work for. Operating in the software and digital media industries, 
Adobe’s major achievements include creating iconic tools like Photoshop, Illustrator, and Premiere 
Pro, inventing the PDF format, and expanding into digital marketing and AI-driven creative 
technologies.
